Functional Fitness Assessment

This table was produced with the help of a personal trainer, using the work of Peter Attia and Andy Galpin as a starting point. There is a lot of useful context to be gained from listening to or reading their work in full. This site is not affiliated with them and we do not imply that they would agree with this table. My personal trainer recommends measuring these metrics once every six months. Your personal training plan can then be tailored to focus on areas of relative weakness so you make balanced progress towards your goals. It is critical to avoid injury so please do not attempt these exercises without first engaging the services of a personal trainer or physiotherapist.
